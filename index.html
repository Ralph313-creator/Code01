Performance

The Rappid Data Center provides you the performance and reliability you can count on with a 99.982% (Phil. Site 8) and 99.741% facility uptime guarantee. Work with highly trained network and facilities engineers and benefit from their expertise in monitoring data and server systems around the clock

Reliability

Experience security and site reliability with facilities offering redundant systems such as Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS), precision air cooling, fire suppression, and more. Explore a wide range of solutions such as ethernet, leased line, frame relay, and more to enjoy elevated connectivity.

High Security

Rely on extremely high-security measures from authentication to points of entry to controlled and supervised access, and more.

Highly Compliant

Adhere to compliance and regulation standards such as International Organization of Standardization (ISO), Payment Card Industry-Data Security Standard (PCI-DSS) and more.
